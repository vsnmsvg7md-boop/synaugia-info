Privacy Policy — SYNAUGIA

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

SYNAUGIA ("the App") is an intelligent harmony tool for musicians. This policy explains our data protocols following the integration of cloud-based authentication and storage systems.

1. Data Collection

To facilitate persistent user sessions and cross-device synchronization, we process:

  • [AUTH_DATA]: Email address and display name via Firebase Authentication.
  • [USER_DATA]: Musical sequences and theoretical analyses stored in Google Cloud Firestore.
  • [META_DATA]: Device identifiers collected by Firebase for security auditing.

2. Processing & Security

Data is routed through secure protocols (HTTPS). Musical inputs are transmitted to specialized AI models via an intermediary proxy server to protect API integrity. No persistent user identity is linked during these external API calls.

3. Retention & Deletion

In compliance with international privacy standards and App Store guidelines:

  • Users maintain full access to modify their profile metadata (display name) within the App.
  • Account Deletion: Users may trigger a permanent wipe of their account and all associated Firestore records via the settings menu. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

4. Jurisdiction

Operations are governed by the laws of France. International data transfers are secured under standard contractual clauses.
