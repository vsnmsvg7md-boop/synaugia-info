This document outlines the protocols for data handling following the integration of cloud-based authentication and storage systems. ## 01_DATA_COLLECTION To facilitate persistent user sessions and cross-device synchronization, the following data points are processed: * **[AUTH_DATA]** : Email address and display name via Firebase Authentication. * **[USER_DATA]** : Musical sequences, chord progressions, and theoretical analyses stored in Google Cloud Firestore. * **[META_DATA]** : Device identifiers and IP addresses collected by Firebase for security auditing and rate limiting. ## 02_PROCESSING_NODES Data is routed through the following infrastructures: * **[FIREBASE]** : Primary data controller for authentication and database management. * **[AI_SERVICES]** : Musical inputs are transmitted via secure protocols to specialized AI models for harmonic processing. No persistent identity is linked during these API calls. ## 03_RETENTION_&_DELETION In compliance with Apple App Store requirements and global privacy laws: * Users maintain full access to modify their profile information within the App. * **[PURGE_PROTOCOL]** : Account deletion requests via the App settings trigger a permanent wipe of associated Firestore documents and Auth records within 30 days. ## 04_JURISDICTION Operations are governed by the laws of France. International data transfers to Google Cloud servers are secured under standard contractual clauses. --- *END_OF_DOCUMENT*
